About the symposium
Symposium Endogyneco

Santa Joana Recife Hospital and the Santa Joana Institute of Education and Research (ISEP), as the sponsoring institutions, are honored to present Endogineco Recife. This event's main purpose is to strengthen endoscopic gynecology by promoting scientific updates, knowledge dissemination, and the exchange of experiences among specialists, opinion leaders, and professionals in training. The meeting aims to further improve care for women with surgically treatable gynecological conditions, such as endometriosis and other conditions. Our program will feature renowned specialists and live-streamed surgeries, providing a practical and enriching experience. The event is aimed at gynecologists, obstetricians, residents, and multidisciplinary team members from throughout Brazil.
